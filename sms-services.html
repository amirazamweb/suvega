Top reasons why to choose Suvega Digital Media for SMS Services

Personalised SMSes

Inserting a first name, last name, or custom field to your SMS messages not just makes your audience feel valued, but also increases the effectiveness of your campaign. Send personalised text for better results.

HTTP/XML SMPP API

SMPP Connectivity has redefined the SMS services and enabled the companies to relay their messages efficiently. SMS APIs such as HTTP, SMTP and XML are popular and SMPP is preferable for large volume SMSes

Multiple SMS Campaigns

Run multiple SMS Campaigns together seamlessly. Our SMS service is robust enough to handle such requirements and deliver efficient performance.

Analytics Dashboard

Track and control your SMS messaging results with our unique yet easy to use dashboards. Gain insights into SMS delivery, compliance status and ROI and much more.

DND Check

DND is a regulation by TRAI to exempt Telecom users from unsolicited SMS/Calls. We can ascertain prior whether mobile number is registered as DND customer with NCPR database.

Instant Delivery Reports

Measure campaign outreach and effectiveness with real-time SMS delivery reports which can also be downloaded in an MS-Excel worksheet for more profound analysis.

Trans-Promo Routes

Transactional SMS refers to SMS related to transactions like OTP, bank or any other non-promotional information. Promotional SMS refers to SMS sent for promoting or advertising your brand.

International Delivery

International SMS is the best way to send and receive instant, reliable communications from your overseas clients and customers – even when they don’t have internet access.

Exceptional Throughput

SMS throughput is the measure of data transfer between connections measured by message per second. Higher throughput enables us to deliver timely messages even during peak times.
