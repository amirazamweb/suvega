FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WhatsApp Business API enables businesses to chat with their customers in the same way they chat with their friends and family – with fast, simple, and secure messaging. Enterprises can integrate customer engagement platforms with WhatsApp through the API and reply to their customers promptly with messages, notifications, shipping confirmations, appointment reminders or event tickets.

Businesses of all sizes looking to provide their customers with a direct channel of communication, prompt support, and an efficient customer experience are eligible to use the WhatsApp Business API.

You can use the WhatsApp Business API for a large number of Trans-promotional cases. These include but are not limited to: travel updates and ticket confirmation, in-message payment links and receipts, order placements, reservations, delivery updates, appointment reminders, and direct customer communication enabling your team to offer efficient and personalized support.

To start using the WhatsApp Business API, you need to submit a request. You can submit a request via Facebook or WhatsApp solution providers like Suvega Digital After your application is approved and your business account is created, you can use solution providers to connect with WhatsApp users. Suvega Digital offers a business chat and conversational commerce solution that easily integrates the WhatsApp Business API.

To ensure the solution is running to the best of its capacity, the WhatsApp Business API is currently available to a limited public view and WhatsApp approval is required for all customers entering the program.

Replies to customer-initiated messages are free over the WhatsApp Business API. However, any communication outside of a 24-hour window from the first message will come at a fixed cost per message, depending on the country.
